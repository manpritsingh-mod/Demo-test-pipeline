🎨 Styling Tips

Make the hackathon box visually distinct from regular bullets:

✅ Use gold/yellow background
✅ Add a trophy 🏆 icon
✅ Use a dashed border
✅ Keep text short — 2-3 lines max
❌ Don't make it bigger than other columns
❌ Don't use red — save that for challenges

💡 Why This Placement Works

Directors scan slides in a Z-pattern — top-left to top-right, then bottom-left to bottom-right. Placing the hackathon at the bottom of Achievements means:

1. They see your core work FIRST
2. Then the hackathon catches their
   eye as a BONUS achievement
3. The golden color creates a
   "pleasant surprise" moment
4. It says: "This intern goes
   above and beyond"
